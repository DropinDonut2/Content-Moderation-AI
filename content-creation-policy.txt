7. Zero Tolerance Violations
CRITICAL: These result in IMMEDIATE PERMANENT BAN
You MUST escalate these immediately - do not just reject.
7.1 Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) / Minor Sexualization
REJECT if content includes:
Any sexualization of minors (under 18)
Characters appearing to be minors in sexual/NSFW contexts
NSFW characters with minor-like features:
Child-like faces or body proportions
Youthful/adolescent appearance
School-age characteristics
Baby faces, small statures
Regardless of stated age in description
Red Flags:
"18 year old high school student" with child-like appearance in NSFW context
"Petite" or "looks young" characters in NSFW storylines
School uniforms in NSFW content
Age statements like "just turned 18" with youthful depiction
Anime "loli" or "shota" style characters in NSFW
Important: Visual appearance matters more than stated age. If a character looks underage in NSFW content, reject and escalate.

7.2 Non-Consensual Sexual Content
REJECT if storyline/character describes:
Rape or sexual assault
Sexual coercion or manipulation
Drugging for sexual purposes
Kidnapping for sexual purposes
Any sexual activity without clear consent
Red Flags:
"Forces," "makes," "doesn't take no for an answer"
Scenarios where player character is restrained/trapped in sexual context
"Surprise" sexual encounters without consent setup
Power imbalances exploited for sex (captive, unconscious, etc.)

7.3 Hate Speech & Discrimination
REJECT if content:
Promotes hatred toward protected groups
Uses slurs or derogatory language toward races, religions, sexual orientations, etc.
Dehumanizes people based on identity
Denies genocides or violent historical events
Mocks or demeans cultural/religious practices maliciously
Examples:
Nazi symbolism glorification
Holocaust denial
Racial slurs in character descriptions
Religious mockery promoting hatred
Anti-LGBTQ+ hate content

7.4 Other Zero Tolerance Content
Terrorism/Violent Extremism: Content promoting terrorist organizations or acts
Self-Harm Promotion: Content encouraging suicide, self-harm, or eating disorders
Bestiality: Sexual content involving animals
Incest Themes: Sexual content between family members

Escalation Protocol for Zero Tolerance
DO NOT APPROVE under any circumstances
Mark as: ZERO TOLERANCE VIOLATION
Use standardized message (no detailed explanation)
ESCALATE to senior moderator within 1 hour
Document in incident log:
Submission ID

Violation type
Time of discovery
Senior moderator will initiate ban process

EC-1: Fan Fiction & Copyright
Issue: Creator submits storyline/character based on or inspired by existing IP (Harry Potter, Marvel, Warhammer 40k, anime, etc.)

Monetization Decision Framework:
DEMONETIZE (No Profit Sharing) if:
‚ùå Text uses copyrighted elements:
Copyrighted character names (e.g., "Harry Potter", "Iron Man", "Goku", "Space Marines")
Copyrighted locations (e.g., "Hogwarts", "Stark Tower", "Konohagakure", "Terra")
Copyrighted organization names (e.g., "Avengers", "Akatsuki", "Adeptus Astartes")
Copyrighted terminology unique to that IP (e.g., "Quirks" from My Hero Academia)
100% direct copy or obvious reference to existing IP
‚ùå Images contain copyrighted elements:
Official character art from anime/manga/games/movies
Recognizable copyrighted character designs (even if renamed)
Copyrighted logos or symbols
Screenshots from copyrighted media
Fan art of copyrighted characters
Examples requiring demonetization:
‚ùå "You are Harry Potter at Hogwarts"
‚ùå "Join the Avengers with Iron Man"
‚ùå Original name "Alex"(example) but image shows Iron Man suit
‚ùå "Satoshi the ninja" but image is clearly Naruto
‚ùå Original story but uses Avengers logo in image

MONETIZABLE (Profit Sharing Allowed) if:
‚úÖ "Inspired by" with ALL original elements:
Similar concepts/settings but ORIGINAL names
Transformative work with NO direct copyrighted references
New characters, new locations, new organizations
Original or generic images (not recognizable copyrighted designs)
No copyrighted logos or symbols

Examples allowed for monetization (Inspired Work):
‚úÖ Warhammer-inspired: "30th Battalion"(not an existing copyrighted material) with original names/locations (not existing chapters/legions)
‚úÖ MHA-inspired: "Your Hero Academia" with original characters, original school, original powers
‚úÖ Marvel-inspired: "Superhero team" with original hero names and original/generic superhero images
‚úÖ Harry Potter-inspired: "Magic school" with original school name, original characters, original images
The Complete Monetization Rule: For content to be MONETIZABLE, ALL must be true:
‚úÖ No copyrighted character names
‚úÖ No copyrighted location names
‚úÖ No copyrighted organization names
‚úÖ No copyrighted unique terms
‚úÖ No copyrighted character images
‚úÖ No copyrighted logos/symbols

If ANY element uses copyrighted material ‚Üí Demonetize
The Recognition Test:
"If you removed context, could someone still identify the exact copyrighted work?"
YES ‚Üí Demonetize (too direct)
NO ‚Üí Can monetize (sufficiently transformative)
"Would the IP holder recognize their property in this content?"
YES ‚Üí Demonetize
NO ‚Üí Monetizable

Original/generic art? ‚Üí Continue to Step 3
Step 3: Make Decision
‚úÖ APPROVE content if meets quality/policy standards
‚ö†Ô∏è DEMONETIZE if ANY copyrighted elements found
‚úÖ MONETIZABLE if ALL elements are original/transformative
Step 4: Document Add note specifying reason:

AI-Generated Images That Are Too Similar
Issue: Creator uses AI-generated image (not official art), but the result is 90%+ similar to copyrighted character(s) and difficult to distinguish.
Rule: Even if AI-generated, if the likeness is too close to distinguish from the original copyrighted character ‚Üí Demonetize
Why: The image is still effectively a reproduction of copyrighted character design, regardless of generation method.
Examples:
‚ùå AI-generated image that looks exactly like Naruto (orange jumpsuit, headband, whisker marks, blonde spiky hair) ‚Üí Demonetize
‚ùå AI-generated image 90% similar to Goku (orange gi, spiky black hair, same face/body structure) ‚Üí Demonetize
‚úÖ AI-generated blonde spiky-haired ninja in different outfit/style ‚Üí May be monetizable
‚úÖ AI-generated warrior with some similar features but clearly different design ‚Üí Monetizable

The Test:
"If you showed this image to fans of the original IP, would they immediately recognize it as [character name]?"
YES (90%+ similar, hard to distinguish) ‚Üí Demonetize
NO (inspired but clearly different) ‚Üí Monetizable
Moderator note: AI generation isn't exempt from copyright concerns. Judge the final output, not the creation method.

‚ö†Ô∏è Demonetization is NOT rejection or punishment

Fan fiction itself is NOT a rejection reason
