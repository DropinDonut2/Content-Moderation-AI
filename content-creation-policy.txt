Content Creation Policy

Last Updated: 15 DECEMBER 2025 

 

 This Content Creation Policy applies to ISEKAI ZERO services (the "Platform"), which include all apps, websites, software, and related services accessed via any platform or device that are linked to this Content Creation Policy. The Platform is provided and controlled by ARx Media Sdn. Bhd. ("ISEKAI ZERO", "we", "our" or "us"). 

 This Content Creation Policy is designed to keep our community of creators and players protected, and to encourage creators to take responsibility for their part in this effort. It sets out what is and is not allowed on the Platform, and applies to all content across the platform. 

 This Content Creation Policy applies to all types of creator-authored content on the Platform, including Character profiles, Storyline descriptions, images, thumbnails, first messages, comments, metadata, and any other content directly created and controlled by users. These guidelines are designed to protect the community from harmful content, harassment, and spam, and are regularly updated in consultation with external experts and ISEKAI ZERO creators. 

 

 Scope of Creator Responsibility 

 This policy applies to content that creators directly author and control. As an AI-powered platform, it's important to distinguish between content creators are responsible for and content generated by the AI system during gameplay. 

 Creators Are Responsible For: 

 Character profiles - Names, descriptions, personality traits, backgrounds, appearance descriptions 

 

 

 Storyline content - Titles, descriptions, synopsis, plot summaries, premises 

 

 

 Storyline cover images - Thumbnails, cover artwork 

 

 

 First messages/Opening scenarios - The initial scene or message that begins a Storyline 

 

 

 Character images - Avatar images, character artwork, visual designs 

 

 

 Tags and metadata - SFW/NSFW classification, age ratings, content warnings, genre tags 

 

 

 Comments - Any comments creators post on the platform 

 

 

 Collections - Curated collections of Storylines or Characters 

 

 

 External links - Any links included in descriptions or profiles 

 

 

 

 Creators Are NOT Responsible For: 

 

 

 AI-generated dialogue - The actual responses generated by the AI during player interactions 

 

 

 Player inputs - What players type or how they interact with Characters 

 

 

 Unexpected AI behavior - Unintended responses that occur despite appropriate character design 

 

 

 Important Note: While you are not responsible for real-time AI responses, you ARE responsible for how you design and set up your Characters and Storylines. Content deliberately designed to circumvent policies or generate violations will be treated as a policy violation. 

 

 1. Spam, Scams and Deceptive Practices Policies 

 

 

 

 

 

 Character/Storyline Spam 

 

 

 Content that is excessively posted, repetitive, or untargeted and does one or more of the following: 

 

 

 Character descriptions or Storyline synopsis that promise players something but deliver different content. 

 

 

 Gets clicks, views, or traffic off the Platform by promising viewers that they will make quick money. 

 

 

 Sends audiences to sites that spread harmful software, try to gather personal information, or other sites that have negative impacts. 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 Misleading thumbnails or metadata 

 

 

 Using the Character names, descriptions, images, Storyline titles, or cover images to trick users into believing the content is something it is not what it is. 

 

 

 

 

 Incentivization spam 

 

 

 Content that sells engagement metrics such as views, likes, comments, or any other metric on the Platform. This type of spam can also include content where the only purpose is to boost subscribers, views, or other metrics. 

 

 

 

 

 Comments spam 

 

 

 Comments where the sole purpose is to gather personal info from viewers, misleadingly drive viewers off ISEKAI ZERO, or perform any of the prohibited behaviors noted above. 

 

 

 

 

 Repetitive comments 

 

 

 Leaving large amounts of identical, untargeted or repetitive comments. 

 

 

 

 

 Scams 

 

 

 Content offering cash gifts, pyramid schemes or “get rich quick” schemes to lure users. 

 

 

 

 

 3rd party content 

 

 

 Character profiles or Storyline descriptions directly copied from other creators without permission or significant modification. Creators should actively ensure their content is original or properly attributed. 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 2. Sexual and Nudity Content Policy

 The depiction of exposed genitalia in Character images or Storyline covers (illustrated, AI-generated, or photographic) that are meant for sexual gratification.

 Character descriptions or Storyline setups involving incest themes. 

 

 

 Character descriptions or Storyline setups involving bestiality. 

 

 

 Pornography or fetishes in Character images, cover images, or opening scenarios that are meant for sexual gratification. 

 

 

 This policy applies to Character profiles, Character images, Storyline descriptions, Storyline covers, first messages, comments, metadata, and any other creator-authored content. Remember these are just some examples, and don't post content if you think it might violate this policy. 

 What is Allowed with Proper NSFW Tagging (18+ only): 

 

 

 Suggestive Character descriptions without explicit detail 

 

 

 Romantic or intimate Storyline scenarios (non-explicit) 

 

 

 Character images with artistic/partial nudity that does not show genitalia 

 

 

 Mature themes in Storyline premises (within boundaries above) 

 

 

 Adult language in Character personalities or Storyline descriptions 

 

 

 NSFW Tagging Requirements: 

 

 

 All NSFW Character profiles and Storylines must be properly tagged upon creation 

 

 Character images and Storyline cover images may contain suggestive or mildly NSFW content, provided they do not include: - Exposed genitalia (illustrated, AI-generated, or photographic) - Explicit sexual acts or simulations - Graphic nudity meant for sexual gratification - Content that would violate the Thumbnails and Images Policy (Section 6)

 3. External Links Policy 

 Do not post links in your Character descriptions, Storyline descriptions, comments, or any creator-authored content that direct users to content that violates our Policies. This includes links that are as stated but not limited to the following: 

 

 

 Links to pornography. 

 

 

 Links to websites or apps that install malware. 

 

 

 Links to websites or apps phishing for a user's sign in info, financial info, etc. 

 

 

 Links to websites, apps, or other sources that give unauthorized access to audio content, audio-visual content, video games, software, or streaming services that normally require payment. 

 

 

 Links to websites that seek to raise funds or recruit for terrorist organizations. 

 

 

 Links to sites containing Child Sexual Abuse Imagery (CSAI). 

 

 

 Links to content containing hate or harassment elements. 

 

 

 Links to content encouraging others to commit violent acts. 

 

 

 Links to websites or apps that spread misleading or deceptive content that can cause serious risk of egregious harm, such as interfering with democratic processes. 

 

 

 This Policy applies to Character profiles, Storyline descriptions, comments, and any other creator-authored content on the Platform. Links can take any form that would direct a user to a site off the Platform. These links include: clickable URLs, showing text of URLs in videos or images, and obfuscated URLs (such as writing “dot com” instead of “.com”). These links can also include verbally directing users to other sites, encouraging viewers to visit creator profiles or pages on other sites, or promising violative content on other sites. This list is not complete. Please note that affiliate content does not violate the Platform’s Terms of Use. Excessively posting affiliate content in dedicated accounts may violate our policies around spam. 

 

 4. Age-Restricted Content 

 We may add an age restriction to creator-authored content that includes any of the following: 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 Harmful or dangerous acts that minors could imitate 

 

 

 Character descriptions or Storyline setups containing adults participating in dangerous activities that minors could easily imitate. 

 

 

 

 

 Adult themes in family content 

 

 

 Content meant for adult audiences but could easily be confused with family content. This includes Characters or Storylines that contain adult themes such as violence, sex, or death. Please age restrict your content upon upload if it is intended for mature audiences. 

 

 

 

 

 Vulgar languages 

 

 

 Content using sexually explicit language or excessive profanity may lead to age restriction as some languages are not appropriate for younger audiences. 

 

 

 

 

 

 If your Character description or Storyline setup warns minors not to perform dangerous activities, we may allow this content without restriction. It will also need to explain the need for professional adult supervision. 

 

 5. Child Safety Policy 

 This policy applies to Character profiles, Character images, Storyline descriptions, Storyline covers, first messages, comments, and any other creator-authored content. Do not post content on the Platform if it fits any of the following: 

 

 

 Inflicting or advocating for the infliction of physical, sexual or emotional maltreatment or neglect of a child in Character descriptions, Storyline premises, or images, including inflicting emotional distress on minors. 

 

 

 Content that contains infliction of physical, sexual, or emotional abuse of a child within an educational, documentary, scientific, or artistic context and with blurring may receive an exception. 

 

 

 Content in Character descriptions or Storyline setups that could cause minor participants or viewers emotional distress, including: 

 

 

 

 Exposing minors to mature themes 

 

 

 Simulating parental abuse 

 

 

 Coercing minors 

 

 

 Violence 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 Sexualization of minors 

 

 

 Sexually explicit content featuring minors and content that sexually exploits minors including minor nudity posted with comedic intent. 

 This includes NSFW characters with minor-like appearances, child-like features, or youthful characteristics, regardless of stated age. Characters tagged NSFW  must clearly appear as adults in all visual depictions and descriptions. 

 

 

 

 

 Harmful or dangerous acts involving minors 

 

 

 Character descriptions or Storyline setups showing a minor participating in dangerous activities or encouraging minors to do dangerous activities. Never create content that puts minors in harmful situations that may lead to injury, including dangerous stunts, challenges, dares, or pranks. 

 

 

 

 

 Misleading family content 

 

 

 Character profiles or Storyline descriptions that appear suitable for children but contain: 

 

 

 Sexual themes. 

 

 

 Violence. 

 

 

 Obscenity or other mature themes not suitable for young audiences. 

 

 

 Medical procedures. 

 

 

 Self-harm. 

 

 

 Use of adult horror characters. 

 

 

 Other inappropriate themes intended to shock young audiences. 

 

 

 Content that targets young minors and families with age-inappropriate themes within an educational, documentary, scientific, or artistic context may receive an exception. This is not a pass to target young minors and families with mature themes intended to shock young audiences. 

 

 

 Content labeled as suitable for kids in the Character/Storyline title, description, tags, or metadata that contains age-inappropriate themes such as violence, sex, death, drugs, and more. Content labelled as suitable for kids in the video's title, description, tags, or in the audience selection if it contains age-inappropriate themes are not allowed. 

 

 

 Make sure your titles, descriptions, and tags match the audience you're targeting. In addition, ensure your audience selection accurately represents the audience your content is suitable for. You can also age restrict your content upon upload if it’s intended for mature audiences. 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 Cyberbullying and harassment involving minors 

 

 

 

 

 Intends to shame, deceive or insult a minor. 

 

 

 Reveals personal information like email addresses or bank account numbers. 

 

 

 Contains sexualization. 

 

 

 Encourages others to bully or harass.

 7. Suicide, Self-Harm and Eating Disorders Policy 

 At ISEKAI ZERO, we take the health and well-being of all our creators, users and viewers seriously. Awareness and understanding of mental health is important and we support creators sharing their stories, such as posting content discussing their experiences with depression, self-harm, eating disorders, or other mental health issues. However, we do not allow content on the Platform that promotes suicide, self-harm, or eating disorders, that is intended to shock or disgust, or that poses a considerable risk to viewers. If you find yourself being negatively affected by any mental health, suicide, self-harm, or eating disorder related content you come across, know that there is support available and you are not alone.

 13. Elections Misinformation Policy 

 Do not create content on the Platform if it fits any of the following: 

 

 

 

 

 

 Voter suppression 

 

 

 Content aiming to mislead voters about the time, place, means, or eligibility requirements for voting, or false claims that could materially discourage voting. 

 

 

 

 

 Candidate eligibility 

 

 

 Content that advances false claims related to the technical eligibility requirements for current political candidates and sitting elected government officials to serve in office. Eligibility requirements considered are based on applicable national law, and include age, citizenship, or vital status. 

 

 

 

 

 Incitement to interfere with democratic processes 

 

 

 Content encouraging others to interfere with democratic processes. This includes obstructing or interrupting voting procedures. 

 

 

 

 

 Election integrity 

 

 

 Content advancing false claims that widespread fraud, errors, or glitches occurred in certain past elections to determine heads of government or content that claims that the certified results of those elections were false. 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 14. Medical Misinformation Policy 

 Do not create content on the Platform if it fits any of the following: 

 

 

 

 

 

 Prevention misinformation 

 

 

 Character descriptions or Storyline setups that promote information that contradicts health authority guidance on the prevention or transmission of specific health conditions, or on the safety, efficacy or ingredients of currently approved and administered vaccines. 

 

 

 

 

 Treatment misinformation 

 

 

 Content in Character profiles or Storyline descriptions that promotes information that contradicts health authority guidance on treatments for specific health conditions, including promotion of specific harmful substances or practices that have not been approved by local health authorities or the World Health Organization as safe or effective, or that have been confirmed to cause severe harm. 

 

 

 

 

 Denial misinformation 

 

 

 Character descriptions or Storyline setups that deny the existence of specific health conditions. 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 15. Educational, Documentary, Scientific, and Artistic (EDSA) Content 

 ISEKAI ZERO allows certain creator-authored content that might otherwise violate guidelines if it offers compelling reasons and context for players. This is known as the EDSA exception, and we evaluate such content based on the following factors: 

 a. Copyright and Intellectual Property 

 ISEKAI ZERO's policies include respecting copyright and intellectual property laws. Creators are responsible for ensuring that their Character profiles, Storyline descriptions, and images do not violate copyright laws. Please keep in mind that: 

 

 

 Sometimes you can use someone else's content without infringing their copyright, such as in cases of fair use. You can also use someone else's content when you have permission from them. 

 

 

 If you create a Character or Storyline that contains someone else's work, your content could be removed due to a copyright takedown request. 

 

 

 If your copyright-protected content was posted on the Platform without your permission, you can submit a copyright removal request to contact@isekai.world. 

 

 

 ISEKAI ZERO does not decide copyright ownership or mediate ownership disputes. 

 

 

 Fan fiction and transformative works are generally allowed but must not violate other policies. 

 

 

 

 b. Enforcement and Compliance 

 ISEKAI ZERO uses a combination of human reviewers and machine learning to enforce its policies. The platform also allows the community to flag content they find inappropriate. ISEKAI ZERO's approach to enforcement includes age-restricting content that may not be suitable for users under 18 years of age. 

 What We Moderate: 

 

 

 Public creator-authored content (Character profiles, Storyline descriptions, images, metadata) is subject to AI flagging and human moderator review 

 

 

 Private/unlisted creator-authored content is not proactively moderated unless reported for illegal content 

 

 

 AI-generated chat responses are not proactively monitored
